Frequently
Asked Questions!

Have questions about Visit Kosovo? We've got you covered! Here are some common inquiries about our platform, services, and how we help you explore Kosovo with ease.

What is Visit Kosovo?

add

Visit Kosovo is your ultimate online guide to discovering the beauty, culture, and experiences of Kosovo. We provide travel tips, must-see destinations, local insights, and exclusive recommendations to make your trip unforgettable.

Is Visit Kosovo a travel agency?

add

Yes! Visit Kosovo helps you discover and book amazing trips across Kosovo. We showcase top destinations with pricing, making it easy to plan your perfect getaway.

How do I book a trip through Visit Kosovo?

add

Simply browse our listed destinations, check the prices, and follow the provided booking instructions. Some trips can be booked directly through our platform, while others may require contacting the travel provider.

What types of destinations do you offer?

add

We feature a variety of destinations, including cultural landmarks, natural attractions, city tours, and adventure trips. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing retreat or an adrenaline-filled experience, we have something for you!

Is transportation included in the prices?

add

It depends on the destination. Some packages include transportation, while others only cover accommodations and activities. Be sure to check the details on each listing for what's included.
